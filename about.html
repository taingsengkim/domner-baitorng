Our story

Born from a Passion
for Exploration

Domner BaiTorng was founded in 2012 by two adventurers who believed that truly meaningful travel should be both effortless and transformative. What started as a small Portland-based agency has grown into a global team of 60+ travel specialists.

We don't just book trips — we craft narratives. Every itinerary is built around authentic local experiences and responsible tourism practices.
