Quick answers

Common Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3 months ahead, especially for peak season. Last-minute trips are possible but subject to availability.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes — groups of 6+ receive 10% off any package. Larger groups can request custom quotes with further savings.

What's included in your packages?

All packages include accommodation, guided tours, and transfers. Flights and meals vary — check each package for details.

What is your cancellation policy?

Full refund up to 45 days before departure. 50% refund up to 21 days. We strongly recommend travel insurance for all bookings.
